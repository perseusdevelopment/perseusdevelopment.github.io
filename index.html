About Me

North-East Native, Avid Surfer, and Amature Master Chef. I can be found reading science fiction novels on Sundays after exploring a new brunch spot. I am happiest when by the water, whether it be a lake, river, or my favortie the beach.

I found my way to the development field by accident after years in the service industry. I was tasked with creating a web page for a high end restaurant, and at the time I had no idea how to go about this. After hours of YouTube videos and trial and error I created my first webpage. I was hooked, I continued to learn more and more until I finally realized this is what I wanted to do in life, create.

Since then I have enrolled and completed an intensive full stack development boot camp. It opened up my eyes to how many niches the development field truly has. I continue to strive everyday to learn something new to help me become a better developer and contribute more to jobs and contracts I work on.
